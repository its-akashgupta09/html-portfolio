The Best Movies According to Akash

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

This is one of my favourite movies. It highlights the importance of following one’s passion and explains the true purpose of life beyond societal pressure and success.

The Return of Abhimanyu

This movie is very engaging as it focuses on the risks of sharing personal information on the internet. It shows how hackers track online activity and exploit data to carry out cybercrimes and harm individuals.

Dhurandhar

This movie was released recently and is based on events involving terrorist attacks and conspiracies against India. It showcases how Indian RAW agents successfully uncover and foil these threats, protecting the nation from terrorists. Overall, it is an intense and impactful thriller.
